About the project

KALD Digital Archive

A structured, searchable, and access-controlled archive platform for institutional photos, videos, documents, reports, and historical media.

Purpose

Organize scattered institutional media and records into one controlled archive.

Security

Public pages contain no archive listings. Authorized access is handled by the secure Apps Script portal.

Technology

Google Apps Script, Google Drive, Python automation, static hosting, audit reports, and controlled deployment workflows.
