About the project

KALD Digital Archive

A structured archive platform for faster discovery of institutional photos, videos, documents, reports, and historical media.

Purpose

Transform scattered media and records into a searchable, organized, and maintainable archive.

Technology

Google Apps Script, Google Drive, Python automation, static web technologies, and controlled deployment workflows.

Public-safe design

The showcase demonstrates the project while keeping original archive content and access credentials private.

This showcase is not the private archive. It is the professional presentation layer for clients, owners, and project reviewers.
